Mars

Name Origin: Mars is named after the Roman god of war.

Known since: Observed by ancient astronomers.

Average distance from Earth: 140 million miles

Things to know:

  • One day is 24h 37m.
  • One year is 687 Earth days.
  • Gravity is 38% of Earth's.
  • Has 2 moons: Phobos & Deimos.

Atmosphere:

CO₂: 95.97%

N₂: 1.89%

Ar: 1.93%

O₂: 0.146%

Interesting Facts:

  • Mars experiences the largest dust storms in the solar system, sometimes enveloping the entire planet.
  • Mars’s moon Phobos is gradually spiraling inward and may break apart into a ring in 10-50 million years.
