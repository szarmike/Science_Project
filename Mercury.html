Mercury

Name Origin: Mercury is named after the Roman messenger god.

Known since: Observed by ancient astronomers.

Average distance from Earth: 48 million miles

Things to know:

  • One day is 58.6 Earth days.
  • One year is 88 Earth days.
  • Gravity is 38% of Earth's.
  • Has no moons.

Atmosphere:

O: 42%

Na: 29%

H: 22%

He: 6%

K: 0.5%

Interesting Facts:

  • Mercury has water ice in permanently shadowed craters at its poles.
  • Mercury's magnetic field is offset, with its dipole nearly 20% south of the planet's center.
